Thank you for choosing Privent Clothing. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how Privent Clothing collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, interact with our mobile applications, or engage with our services.

By accessing or using our website or services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our website or services.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website or use our services, we may collect certain information about you, including:

  • Personal Information: When you make a purchase, register for an account, sign up for our newsletter, or contact us for support, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and payment information.
  • Browsing Information: We may collect information about your browsing behavior on our website, including the pages you visit, the products you view, and the links you click.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our website or services, including the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect information about your precise or approximate location using GPS, Wi-Fi, or mobile network information.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our website and services.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To process and fulfill your orders, provide you with customer support, and communicate with you about your account or transactions.
  • To personalize your experience, tailor our products and services to your interests, and provide you with relevant recommendations and advertisements.
  • To improve our website, products, and services, analyze trends and user behavior, and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, protect the security of our website and services, and comply with legal obligations.
