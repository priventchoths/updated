Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Prevent

General Questions

  1. What is Prevent?
    Prevent is a clothing brand that focuses on combining fashion with purpose and sustainability.
  2. Who are the founders of Prevent?
    Prevent was founded by Jignesh, Rithvik, Sriram, and Shreyas under the mentorship of Karthik Sir and Harish Sir.

Product Questions

  1. What types of clothing does Prevent offer?
    Prevent specializes in t-shirts for men, women, and children.
  2. Are Prevent's t-shirts made from sustainable materials?
    Yes, we prioritize sustainability and use eco-friendly materials in the production of our t-shirts.
  3. Do you offer different styles of t-shirts?
    Yes, we offer various styles including crewneck, V-neck, graphic tees, plain tees, long sleeve, and short sleeve options.

Ordering and Shipping

  1. How can I purchase t-shirts from Prevent?
    You can purchase our t-shirts through our website or visit one of our retail locations.
  2. Does Prevent offer international shipping?
    No, currently we only provide shipping within India.
  3. What are the shipping charges for orders within India?
    Shipping charges vary depending on the location and weight of the package. Please refer to our website for detailed shipping information.
  4. How long does it take for orders to be delivered within India?
    Orders within India typically arrive within 5-7 business days, although delivery times may vary depending on the location.

Returns and Exchanges

  1. What is Prevent's return policy?
    We do not offer returns or exchanges unless the product is damaged or defective upon arrival. Please inspect your order carefully upon delivery and contact us immediately if there are any issues.
  2. How do I report a damaged or defective product?
    If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of receiving your order. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.
