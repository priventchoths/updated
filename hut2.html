About Us

Welcome to Prevent, where fashion meets purpose, and style meets sustainability. Founded under the expert guidance of Karthik Sir and Harish Sir at Learnorama Summer Camp, our brand is more than just a clothing line; it's a movement towards a brighter, more conscious future. With a commitment to innovation, integrity, and inclusivity, we are on a mission to redefine fashion for the modern generation.

Our Origin:
Born from the creative collaboration of four young visionaries – Jignesh, Rithvik, Sriram, and Shreyas – Prevent was cultivated within the nurturing environment of Learnorama Summer Camp. Under the mentorship of Karthik Sir and Harish Sir, we were inspired to channel our passion for design into a meaningful venture aimed at making a positive impact on the world.

Our Philosophy:
At Prevent, we believe in fashion with a purpose. Our designs are not just aesthetically pleasing; they are consciously crafted to promote sustainability and social responsibility. From utilizing eco-friendly materials to supporting fair labor practices, we strive to set a new standard for ethical fashion that empowers both the wearer and the planet.

Our Mentors:
Karthik Sir and Harish Sir are more than just mentors to us; they are beacons of inspiration and pillars of support. Their unwavering guidance and expertise have played a pivotal role in shaping our journey and instilling in us a sense of purpose and responsibility. With their mentorship, we are empowered to push boundaries, challenge norms, and create positive change in the world.

Thank you for choosing Prevent. Here's to a world where fashion doesn't just look good – it does good too.

Warm regards,
